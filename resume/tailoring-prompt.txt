RULES

Each role must include a minimum of eight well-defined skill categories to ensure structural consistency and completeness.
The total number of technologies across all categories must remain within the range of 20 to 25, prioritizing relevance over volume.
Tier-1 (critical) technologies must be positioned within the first two skill categories to maximize recruiter visibility and ATS weighting.
Skill categories must be distinct, non-overlapping, and aligned with the role’s primary engineering domain.
Avoid generic labels. Each category must clearly reflect functional specialization (e.g., “CI/CD & DevOps Tools” instead of “Tools”).
All listed technologies must be supported by demonstrated usage within the experience section to maintain credibility and prevent keyword stuffing.
Maintain ATS-safe formatting using plain text structure. Do not use tables, graphics, or excessive symbols.
